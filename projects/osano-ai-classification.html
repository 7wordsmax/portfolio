Osano

AI Classifications of Cookies & Scripts

Reduce time-to-value for new users by automating the categorization of cookies and scripts for privacy compliance.

The Problem

New users of Osano faced a significant barrier to immediate value: manually categorizing hundreds or thousands of cookies and scripts on their domains. This tedious, error-prone process could take weeks, delaying their ability to achieve compliance and understand their privacy posture.

The Goal

Create an AI-powered system that could automatically detect, classify, and categorize cookies and scripts on a user's domains with minimal human intervention. This would dramatically reduce time-to-value, improve accuracy, and provide an instant snapshot of privacy-relevant technologies across their organization.

Design Approach

We designed a two-phase onboarding experience that introduced AI classification early in the user journey. The system would scan and classify known cookies and scripts, then present results in an intuitive interface where users could review, accept, or override classifications.

Key Features

  • Automatic scanning and detection of cookies and scripts
  • AI-powered classification into privacy categories
  • User review and override capabilities
  • Batch import and one-click acceptance
  • Detailed explanations for each classification

Results & Impact

  • Reduced time-to-value from weeks to hours
  • Increased new user adoption by 40%
  • Improved classification accuracy through hybrid human-AI approach
  • Users gained immediate visibility into their privacy landscape
  • Enabled faster compliance journey for enterprises

Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of reducing friction in critical workflows. By placing the most valuable feature (AI classification) at the beginning of the user journey rather than later, we dramatically improved onboarding outcomes. Users now see immediate value, which increases engagement and retention across the entire platform.
