Osano

AI Driven Compliance Monitor

Automated monitoring system for detecting privacy gaps and compliance risks across customer domains.

The Problem

Even after initial compliance efforts, privacy landscapes constantly evolve. New cookies are added, scripts change, regulations shift. Customers needed continuous monitoring to catch compliance drift before it became a liability. Manual review processes were too slow and error-prone.

The Goal

Build an intelligent monitoring system that continuously scans customer environments, detects changes and gaps, and alerts users to potential compliance issues. This would shift privacy from a one-time project to an ongoing operational discipline.

Design Approach

We designed a dashboard-centric experience that provided at-a-glance compliance health while offering deep-dive analysis capabilities. The system uses AI to prioritize alerts based on severity and business impact, reducing alert fatigue.

Key Features

  • Real-time monitoring of cookies, scripts, and privacy signals
  • AI-powered anomaly and change detection
  • Risk prioritization and severity scoring
  • Historical compliance trends and reporting
  • Automated alerts and remediation workflows

Results & Impact

  • Detected compliance drift weeks before potential violations
  • Reduced time spent on manual privacy audits by 70%
  • Enabled customers to maintain continuous compliance posture
  • Provided audit trail and documentation for regulatory requirements
  • Positioned Osano as a proactive compliance solution

Learnings

This project demonstrated the power of shifting user mindsets from compliance as a project to compliance as an operational practice. By making monitoring continuous and automated, we reduced friction and helped customers stay ahead of regulatory requirements rather than catching up after the fact.
