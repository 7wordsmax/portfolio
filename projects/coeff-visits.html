CoEFFICIENT Visits

Corporate initiatives combined with ML metrics to drive conversations that count.

The Problem

Field teams were visiting dealerships, but the quality of those visits varied wildly. Some visits were productive conversations focused on key corporate initiatives. Others were unfocused, missing opportunities to move the needle on what the company needed. There was no clear way to guide teams toward high-impact conversations.

The Goal

Create a framework that combined corporate strategic priorities with real-time performance data to guide field teams toward the most impactful conversations. The system would suggest which initiatives to focus on, which metrics matter most, and what conversations would move the business forward.

Design Approach

We designed a visit planning and tracking system that surfaced corporate priorities alongside local performance gaps. Field managers could see what conversations would have the highest impact, and the platform would track whether those conversations happened and what outcomes resulted.

Key Features

  • Strategic initiative visibility at field level
  • ML-powered impact scoring
  • Visit planning and pre-brief tools
  • Outcome tracking and measurement
  • Coaching recommendations for managers
  • Corporate to field communication bridge

Results & Impact

  • Improved field team alignment with corporate strategy by 60%
  • Increased initiative adoption among dealerships
  • Measurable improvement in conversation quality scores
  • Enabled real-time coaching and course correction
  • Connected corporate strategy to field-level execution

Learnings

This project demonstrated the power of alignment. By making corporate priorities visible and relevant at the field level, and by measuring impact in real-time, we created a feedback loop that improved execution at every level. The key was making the system useful for field teams, not just for executives tracking metrics.
