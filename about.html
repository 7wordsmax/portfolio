About

I'm a product designer with 10+ years of experience building and scaling products across startups and growth-stage companies. My journey began at Square Root, where I led the design team and helped shape the product strategy for CoEFFICIENT—a field team platform that grew to millions of users and was acquired by CDK Global.

Since then, I've co-founded ROIKOI (acquired by Terminal), and I'm currently the Lead Product Designer at Osano, where I focus on simplifying complex privacy compliance for enterprises using AI and thoughtful design.

I believe great design happens at the intersection of user empathy, business strategy, and technical feasibility. I'm passionate about building design systems that scale, mentoring the next generation of designers, and exploring how AI can enhance—not replace—human-centered design.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me thinking about problem-solving approaches, exploring new technologies, and always looking for ways to make things simpler and better.
